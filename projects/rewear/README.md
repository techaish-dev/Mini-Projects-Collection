# ReWear - Circular Fashion Marketplace

## 🌟 About

ReWear is a circular fashion marketplace that enables users to **rent**, **resell**, and **recycle** clothing in a seamless and sustainable manner. The platform responds to the growing crisis of fast fashion by offering an affordable, eco-conscious alternative.

## 🚀 Features

- **Rent Fashion** - Access designer wear without commitment
- **Resell Items** - Give pre-loved clothes a new home
- **Recycle Garments** - Responsible textile recycling
- **Impact Tracking** - See your environmental contribution
- **Responsive Design** - Works on all devices

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3 (Modern features: Grid, Flexbox, Animations)
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Font Awesome Icons
- Unsplash Images

## 📊 Key Metrics

- 2.1M+ items kept in circulation
- 15K+ active community members
- 3.2K+ tons of waste prevented
- 8.3M+ gallons of water saved

## 🎯 Target Audience

- **Primary:** Students & young adults (eco-aware, budget-conscious)
- **Secondary:** Gen Z & Millennials (digital natives)

## 💡 Business Model

- Rental Commission (10-15% per transaction)
- Resale Fees (flat or percentage based)
- Premium Subscription (AI styling + advanced features)
