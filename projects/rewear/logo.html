Logo Usage Guidelines

Logo Meaning

The ReWear logo combines a recycling symbol with a circular shape, representing the circular fashion economy. The green color symbolizes sustainability and eco-friendliness, while the blue represents trust and reliability.

Logo Variations

The ReWear logo comes in three primary variations: the standard circular logo, a horizontal layout for wider spaces, and a minimal icon for small applications like favicons or social media profiles.

Color Usage

The primary logo should be used in its full color version whenever possible. For single-color applications, use the all-dark or all-white version. Always maintain sufficient clear space around the logo.

Minimum Size

To ensure legibility, the circular logo should never be displayed smaller than 30px in digital formats or 1 inch in print materials.
